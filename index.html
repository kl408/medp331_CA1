Static Website - NONONO

NONONO's website is static due to its simple usage of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript with a lack of databases and limited functionality. Services that may require databases such as deliveries or reservations are linked to other websites instead.

I'm a fan of this site as it is a great way to see the restaurant's ambience and it has great pictures of their food! (I'd recommend their curry udon!) Scrolling through menus could be more accessible however.

Dynamic Website - Discord

Note: The site is much more dynamic when you are logged in.

Discord is a very dynamic application as it holds many functions to connect users to servers. As a messaging platform, it requires complex code and great use of databases.

I use Discord daily and I love how easy it is to navigate through to talk to my friends and edit our own group chats to our preferences!
